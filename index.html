# OMFGPEARS / turntable.fm plug.dj bot
    [Follow @omfgpears](https://twitter.com/omfgpears)
## [What is an OMFGPEARS?](#about) Pears is a rowboat. Due to reasons, some of her commands are different than they were back in the turntable dayz. This page is home base for the pears pears pears pears sparkle sparkle pears pears. She lives here [here](http://plug.dj/tt854).
## [Current Commands](#commands) Commands also work with "pears" prefixed to them unless otherwise noted.
pugme
give me a pug link
dogeme
give me a doge link
lookup _[subject]_
try to find a definition or a wikipedia article on _subject_
similar _[subject]_
give me a search link similar to _subject_
most awesomed/hearted/lamed/played
songs with the most upvotes/snags/mehs/plays
mystats
your personal tt854 stats
cool song
artists similar to the one currently playing via Echonest
_Plus some spooky secret other ones!_
