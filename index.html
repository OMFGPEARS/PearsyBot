Current Commands

Commands also work with "pears" prefixed to them unless otherwise noted.

pugme
give me a pug link
dogeme
give me a doge link
lookup [subject]
try to find a definition or a wikipedia article on subject
similar [subject]
give me a search link similar to subject
most awesomed/hearted/lamed/played
songs with the most upvotes/snags/mehs/plays
mystats
your personal tt854 stats

Plus some spooky secret other ones!
